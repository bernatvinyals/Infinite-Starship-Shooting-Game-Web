How to play

The player has to shoot enemies by pressing Space and moving left and right on the screen. Every time an enemy goes beyond the player reach, meaning that its behind the player, the player score will not be affected.
If one enemy colides with the player, the enemy will disapear and the player will lose 1 HP point.

Goal

Get the maximum amount of points posible.

Controls

Left Arrow Key: Move player to the left.
Up Arrow Key: Move player upwards.
Down Arrow Key: Move player downwards
Right Arrow Key: Move player to the right.
Space: Shoot Misiles.
